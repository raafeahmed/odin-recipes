Keema samosa

Description

Keema samosa is a popular Pakistani snack with a filling of keema enclosed in a flour based pastry and deep fried. Enjoy it hot with mint chutney on the side.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken mince / qeema
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • few drops of oil
  • 1 teaspoon roasted cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 3 - 4 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cups coriander, finely chopped
  • 2 green chili, sliced
  • 40 - 50 samosa patti or spring roll wrappers, cut into required size
  • 4 - 5 tablespoon flour
  • Oil for frying
